A berm a mix between a ferocious bear and a worm - a hybrid of sorts. It's the size of a bear, but tunnels stealthily through the ground and can popup out of anywhere, devouring prey. It has been discovered recently and is believed to be from the thawing permafrost due to rising global temperatures. Another theory is that Berms were caused by underground nuclear testing, the Chernobyl accident, and numerous other nuclear power plant leaks and metldowns. Berms have been observed devouring small animals and even children walking in parks, playing in their yards, or even appearing to burrow through basement walls where time has caused cracks in crumbling foundations.
